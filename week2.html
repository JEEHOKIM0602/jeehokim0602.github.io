Link

In “As We May Think,” Banniver Bush gives scientists directions for scientific developments in the future.
He pondered how to efficiently manage and use tons of information and data. Bush expected the emergence of a
device called “memex,” which uses a human thinking mechanism that links relevant informations or memories
in the head. The memex he describes is very similar to the "WWW" nowadays. Like a web database, data can be
collected and stored through memex. Just as he imagined memex, I noticed that deep thinking about the interaction
between human beings and machines is essential for students learning web development.
