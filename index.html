Auriea Harvey & The Future of Digial Sculpture

Reading Response:

As the Web developed from 1 to 3, there were some changes in the method of how to use them.
Web 1, the earliest stage, provided only a level of service that collects offline information
all around the world so that users could find it digitally.
However, in Web 2, users were able to access other users’ information and share their
information with others. And now, Web 3 allows users to access, upload and share information,
as well as consider their personal information as assets.
NFT is a digital token that uses blockchain technology to protect individual assets in digital space.
In other words, with NFT, Web 3 users are able to maintain the value of their original works and their uniquenesses.
As time changes and technology develops, various forms and methods are emerging in art.
So I think if digital arts are combine with NFTs more, it has a great potential to develop arts in more diverse ways.
